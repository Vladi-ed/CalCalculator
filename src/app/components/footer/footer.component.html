Help

In order to precess and show your credit card transactions please go to Cal Website (desktop version), select desired reporting dates and download the report (press Export button).

Then load the file into the Cal-Calculator App.

You can see your transactions in the table with translation where you can sort and filter your data.

Note: No data transferred anywhere outside of the app. All the data precessing happens locally on your device/browser.

Please contact support in case of failure.
